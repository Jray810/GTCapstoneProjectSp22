Project Description

What is TranzVolt 2.0?

Utilizing technology found in electric vehicles, our team of engineers harnessed the use of a brush-less motor, an electromagnetic brake, and a lithium-ion battery pack, the TranzVolt system is a state of the art ladder hoist popular among roofing and solar panel installation companies (like Tesla!). TranzVolt 2.0 is the second generation in the TranzVolt line, seeking to move greater loads at faster speeds, improving safety mechanisms to protect workers, and improving ease of use for operators.
